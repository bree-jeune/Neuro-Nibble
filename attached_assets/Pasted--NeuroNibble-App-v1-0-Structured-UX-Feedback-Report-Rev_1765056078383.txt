🧠 NeuroNibble App v1.0 - Structured UX Feedback Report
Reviewed as: UX Designer + ADHD/Neurodivergence Subject Matter Expert Review Date: December 6, 2025 Review Scope: Full app walkthrough + user observation notes

📊 Executive Summary
Overall Assessment: The app has strong foundational architecture but suffers from incomplete interaction loops and impersonal user experience. Critical issues center around:
	1	Dead-end interactions (display name saves to nowhere, completed tasks don't archive)
	2	Missing personalization layer (no adaptive learning, no user greeting)
	3	Celebration fatigue (dopamine menu randomization isn't enough celebration)
	4	Body-doubling paradox (claims community feel but has no social features)
	5	Broken task lifecycle (tasks never "complete" properly, causing anxiety)
Priority Fix Order: Onboarding → Task lifecycle → Personalization → Community

Issue 11: App Feels Impersonal
Problem: Despite warm microcopy, the app feels like it's talking AT the user, not WITH them:
	•	No adaptive language ("Your brain is chaotic" vs generic)
	•	No memory of past behavior
	•	No sense that the app "knows" you
Neurodivergent Issue:
	•	ADHD brains need consistency + novelty
	•	Static experience = boring = abandonment
	•	Personal touch = emotional investment = retention

Solution: Personalization Layer
Phase 1: Adaptive Persona (Week 1)
After 7 days of use, app learns:
	•	Typical energy levels (mostly Low? mostly High?)
	•	Stuck patterns (always freezes on "email"?)
	•	Completion rate (finishes bites or abandons?)
App assigns a brain type:
	•	"Chaotic Starter" (high energy, low follow-through)
	•	"Gentle Processor" (low energy, needs small steps)
	•	"Sprint Worker" (high intensity bursts)
Display on "You" profile:

text
Your brain type: Chaotic Starter
You're great at starting. Let's work on the follow-through together.

Phase 2: Adaptive Language (Week 2+)
Example: Home Screen Greeting
Generic (current): "What's freezing you?"
Personalized (after learning): "Hey Bree, I know emails usually get you stuck. Want to break one down together?"

Phase 3: Smart Task Suggestions (Month 1+)
After 30 days:

text
┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
│ You often get stuck on these:       │
│                                     │
│ 💬 Reply to messages                │
│ 📧 Deal with inbox                  │
│                                     │
│ [Pre-shrink these for me]           │
└─────────────────────────────────────┘
App automatically creates 3-bite breakdown for recurring stuck points.
